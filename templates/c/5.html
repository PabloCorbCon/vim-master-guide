At the moment we have seen several special keys to move within the Vim text editor, for example the keys h, j, k or l to move within the editor or u to undo the last change you made to the document.

However the combinations of Vim are endless! There are dozens of special keys in each mode and these can be combined!

In this chapter we are going to look at some of the most used keys and combinations.

  1. - `: move to the last edit
  2. - dw: deletes a word.
  3. - d0: deletes the beginning of a line.
  4. - dgg: deletes until the beginning of the file.
  5. - dG: deletes until the end of the file.
  6. - d$: deletes until the end of the line.
  7. - Ctrl + r: redo the last undo.

I invite you to investigate for yourself other key combinations in Vim.
